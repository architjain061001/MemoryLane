Two Months Yay!

kishu. say hi. it’s a little crazy that i am writing this letter and it’s only been 2 months. it really feels a hell lot more. i feel like i have always known you. i feel like you’ve always been a part of my life. you’re my YJHD dream. you’re that “i was ok until i met you, now i know what it feels like to be happy”. my whole life i have always felt like i have to prove a point to be loved and valued but thank you for helping me feel that i can just be myself and still feel how i feel with you. i genuinely have so much to say, but i also don’t know how to put it. you’re just perfect. you don’t make me feel like i have to work to have you. you make me feel safe. it feels effortless to love you. you really just stick around. you really make me feel ok. and if i had to go through all this pain to finally find peace and be with someone as beautiful as you, i would do it over and over again. and for two people who love cinema, we’ve really written a movie of our own. love you.
